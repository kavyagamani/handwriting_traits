About Us

Who We Are

HandwritingAI is a machine learning-powered system that analyzes handwriting samples to identify personality traits using scientifically studied characteristics such as slant, spacing, baseline alignment, writing pressure, and letter formation patterns.

Our goal is to create an automated and unbiased solution using CNN (MobileNetV2) to provide fast, accurate, and interpretable personality predictions.
